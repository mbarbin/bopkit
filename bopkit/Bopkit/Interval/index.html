Module Bopkit.Interval

type t = {
  1. from : Base.int;
  2. to_ : Base.int;
}

from and to_ are included in the interval. from may be smaller than, equal to, or greater than to_.

When multiple indexes are used in sequence, the development goes from right to left, that is:

     a:[2]:[3] --->  a[0][0], a[0][1], a[0][2], a[1][0], a[1][1], a[1][2]
